Life Insurance – Overview

Bharat National Bank, in partnership with BNB Life Insurance Company, offers a range of life insurance products for Indian families. From pure term plans for maximum coverage at minimal cost, to endowment and ULIP plans combining insurance with wealth creation — we have a solution for every life stage.

Our plans are IRDAI-regulated with a claim settlement ratio of 98.2%, ensuring your family receives promised benefits when they need it most.

🎯 Tax Benefits

Premiums are eligible for deductions up to ₹1.5 Lakhs under Section 80C. Maturity proceeds tax-free under Section 10(10D).

Our Insurance Plans

✓
BNB Term Shield: Pure term life insurance up to ₹2 Crores from ₹499/month. Covers death, critical illness, and accidental disability.
